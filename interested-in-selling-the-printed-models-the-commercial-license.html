Interested in Selling the Printed Models! The Commercial license

We’re happy that some makers and small businesses are interested in selling Dragon Railway models. I would like to let you know that we have a commercial license for those who want to sell Prints of our models while supporting future development.

We first introduced the Commercial membership last year at a member's request.

License details:

  • $100 Monthly Sales: We kindly request you to get the commercial license once your sales from our models exceed 100 USD per month. That way you can get started right away without the costs of upfront licensing.
  • No pressure: This is just a gentle request to those of you who want to add our collection to your 3D printing business. You will support the project's long-term growth with this license.

What the License Covers:

The commercial license allows you to sell physical prints of our premium models. Redistribution of the design files is not allowed. The license is only for selling the physical prints of the models.

Open Source models:

Open source models are freely available for all purposes, including commercial sales. If you're only selling prints of our open-source models, feel free to do so - no strings attached.

However, if you'd like to contribute to the long-term growth and development of Dragon Railway, we kindly encourage you to consider getting the license.

To the sellers and print farms, Add our models to your shop and start selling the prints right away

 

 

Note:

  • We are very flexible & generous with the commercial license for those selling physical prints of our models
  • Reselling the exclusive files is not allowed at any cost - We reserve the right to revoke access when this is violated
